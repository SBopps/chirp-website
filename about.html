About Chirp

Our Story

Chirp started with a phone call from a good friend and executive director of a Texas camp, who needed help navigating the new Heaven 27 regulations. What began as a simple weather detection and alert system quickly grew as conversations with camp directors revealed bigger needs around communication, documentation, and coordination.

Today, Chirp is a full camp safety and critical communications platform — covering weather alerts, emergency broadcasts, staff messaging, and audit logs — all in one place, built to adapt to the way each camp actually operates.

What We're Building

🌦️

Smart Weather Alerts

Hyper-local weather monitoring tied to your camp's GPS coordinates. Receive tornado warnings, lightning alerts, and severe storm notifications before conditions become dangerous. Paid add-on services are available for more complete weather alerting.

📢

Unified Messaging

One platform for all camp communication — staff-to-staff, camp-to-parent, and emergency broadcasts. No more juggling between radios, texts, and email.

✅

Acknowledgment Tracking

Every critical message includes read-receipt confirmation. Know exactly who has seen your alert and follow up with those who haven't.

🚨

Emergency Broadcasting

One-tap emergency alerts that reach every device on campus. Each camp can create their own custom communications protocols.

🗺️

Interactive Camp Map

Live map showing staff positions, facility locations, and emergency assembly points for each particular emergency. Know where everyone is when it matters most.

📋

Compliance & Audit Logs

Automated digital records of every emergency communication — who sent it, who received it, and who acknowledged it. Built for regulatory compliance and peace of mind.

The critical communication platform for camps.
